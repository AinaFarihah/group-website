Negeri Sembilan lies on the western coast of Peninsula Malaysia just south of Kuala Lumpur, and is one of the 13 states of Malaysia. The name literally translates as Nine nations. The Minangkabau people, originally from Western Sumatra, Indonesia, fleeing the attacks from Javanese violently, are thought to have originated from the nine settlements that have been settled there since the 15th Century.


Seremban

A city in Malaysia, in the Malay peninsula's south-western section, in Negeri Sembilan state. 79,900 population (1970). Seremban is an intersection linked to Port Dickson by railway.

Cahaya Negeri Beach,Port Dickson

This is a photo shooting spot. Many people have taken a picture of their wedding here as the landscape here is absolutely breathtaking and surreal. There is a playground for children and even a wooden bridge that has grown into a sight for this beach.


Army Museum,Port Dickson

Historic galleries, an underground tunnel and war equipment, as well as a memorial fountain. It is next to a military base at Port Dickson, a military city for several years and a leading beach.


Upside Down House, Port Dickson

The Upside Down House also houses immersive 3D art that allows tourists to pick up loads of fun and strangen images, all hanging on the ceiling and all upside-down. Visitors are free to get imaginative and use the objects in the house to create original poses and shots out of this world.

P.D. Ostrich Show Farm

PD Ostrich Show Farm was built over a decade ago. It also houses an aquarium for freshwater fish from malaysia. Although the PD Ostrich Show Farm is home to ostrichen, the majority of the population include horses, donkeys, turkeys, ducks and a large range of other friendly animals.
