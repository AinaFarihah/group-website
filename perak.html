Zoo Taiping & Night Safari, Taiping

Taiping Zoo is the first zoo in the country to house over 2,000 animals representing 160 local and exotic species. Visitors can observe these animals in their open-air enclosures during the day or choose a night tour (Night Safari).


The Leaning Tower of Teluk Intan

The Leaning Tower of Teluk Intan (Menara Jam Condong Teluk Intan) is a clock tower in Teluk Intan,Perak, Malaysia. It is the Malaysian equivalent of the world-famous Leaning Tower of Pisa in Italy. The tower is slanted leftward, similar to the Tower of Pisa. It is 25.5 metres tall and, from the outside, looks like an 8 storey building, though inside it is actually divided into 3 storeys.


Pangkor Island

Pangkor Island is a resort island in Manjung District, Perak, Malaysia. It has a population of approximately 25,000. As an island on the western coast of the Peninsula of Malaysia, Pangkor Island is well protected by the monsoon impact. And because of the ideal weather on Pangkor Island that is neither too hot nor too cold, this makes it one of the most popular places to visit all year round in Malaysia.

Mirror Lake, Ipoh

Tasik Cermin, or Mirror Lake, is a stunning hidden lake surrounded by limestone karst towers. It is located in Gunung Rapat, a 20-minute drive from the heart of Ipoh, Perak, Malaysia.
