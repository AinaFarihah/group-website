Clock Tower

Clock tower was constructed to notify the residents who live in this area that it is prayer time. Today, the clock tower still operates as a time-keeper, but it also stands as a reminder of the past as it is one of the oldest Kedah attractions. It is also fascinating to see how this building mixes both Islam and colonial architecture into one.


Kedah Royal Museum

Kedah Royal Museum or locally known as the Sultan Museum, you will be able to see and learn how a Sultan lives behind closed doors. In this place, it houses 2,000 artefacts, documentation and also possessions of Sultan Abdul Halim (former Sultan of Kedah) and his wife.


Ulu Legong Hot Spring

One of the Kedah attractions that topped our list is the Ulu Legong Hot Spring. Always bustling with visitors, this is a perfect place for families to bond and de-stress. Due to the temperature of the hot spring, many people would opt to visit this place in the late evenings.

Payar Island

Payar Island is a government protected marine park in Kedah. Rich with its marine life and colourful reef, snorkelling and diving are some of the most popular activities to do here. There are also other facilities available in this Kedah attraction such as camping and barbeque sites to make your trip complete.
